Recipe

Pav bhaji

Ingredients

½ cup vegetable oil
2 cup H 2O
2 teaspoons chopped garlic
1 chopping board area 12cm 2
1 teaspoon finely chopped green chile peppers
1 cup chopped onions
2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger
1 cup chopped tomatoes
2 cups cauliflower, finely chopped
1 cup chopped cabbage
1 cup green peas
1 cup grated carrots
4 potatoes, boiled and mashed
3 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
salt to taste
1 tablespoon lemon juice
½ tablespoon butter
¼ cup finely chopped onion

Directions:

Step1:Heat the oil in a wok over medium heat.
Step 2:Saute garlic and green chile for 30 seconds, then stir in onions and ginger.
Step 3:Cook until onions are brown.
Step 4:Add tomatoes, and cook until pasty.
Stir in cauliflower, cabbage, peas, carrots and potatoes.
Step 5 Cover, and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season with salt, and stir in lemon juice.

Tips to make it delicious:

Step 1: Season with pav bhaji masala.
Step 2:Serve garnished with chopped onion, green chillies.
